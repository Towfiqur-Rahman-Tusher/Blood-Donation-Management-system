LIFELINE

Saving Lives with Every Drop

Founded in 2023, the LIFELINE Blood Management System was established through the vision of Dr. Akram Evergreen, a dedicated physician committed to revolutionizing blood management and healthcare in Chattogram's Panchlaish area. Nestled in this vibrant community, LIFELINE has swiftly become an essential part of the local healthcare landscape. From its inception, the system was designed to address the critical need for efficient blood donation, storage, and distribution services. Emphasizing personalized care and leveraging the latest in medical technology, LIFELINE Blood Management System has expanded its operations to serve a broader population. Today, it stands as a symbol of innovation and compassion, offering state-of-the-art blood management solutions and unwavering support to all who depend on its services.

OUR VISION

To be the leading provider of compassionate and comprehensive healthcare, enriching lives and inspiring hope in our community.
